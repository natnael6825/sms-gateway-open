## Design Context

### Users
Developers, small businesses, and self-hosters who need to send application SMS through an Android phone and manage the queue from a web dashboard. Their core jobs are deploying the stack, pairing a phone, sending a first message, monitoring delivery, and integrating the HTTP API.

### Brand Personality
Clean, professional, calm, and trustworthy. The product should make infrastructure setup feel understandable without hiding technical details.

### Aesthetic Direction
A refined operations workspace with strong typography, restrained emerald accents, warm neutral surfaces, and intentional data density. Support equally polished light and dark modes. Avoid neon developer styling, generic gradients, and excessive card grids.

### Design Principles
1. Make the next setup action unmistakable.
2. Keep operational status visible and readable at a glance.
3. Reveal technical details progressively and make every credential easy to copy.
4. Use plain language for errors, empty states, and configuration.
5. Preserve keyboard access, visible focus, responsive layouts, and reduced-motion preferences.
